Team Creation — Step by Step

Each team is defined by three ratings that feed directly into the Quick Play Football game engine:

OFF
Offensive rating.
Range: 4–21
DEF
Defensive rating.
Range: 4–21
CGF
Close Game Factor.
Range: 0–12

Step 2 — Roll Stats (2d6 bell curve)

Roll 2d6 for OFF, 2d6 for DEF, 1d6 for CGF. Add the tier modifier. The bell curve means average rolls are most common — extremes are rare.

Tier 2d6 OFF modifier OFF range 2d6 DEF modifier DEF range 1d6 CGF modifier CGF range

Step 3 — Identity Roll (optional d6)

Specialise your team after rolling base stats. This shifts points between OFF and DEF.

d6 Roll Identity Adjustment
1–2OffensiveOFF +2, DEF −2
3–4BalancedNo change
5–6DefensiveOFF −2, DEF +2
